## What Makes Each a Good Reference

Each protocol is preserved here as **exactly registered** on PROSPERO, including all sections:

- **Review title & objectives** — PICO structure, rationale
- **Eligibility criteria** — Population, intervention, comparator, study design, context
- **Search strategy** — Databases, date ranges, language restrictions, screening process
- **Outcomes** — Primary & secondary, measurement tools (GAIS, WSRS, VISIA, Cutometer, etc.)
- **Data extraction & risk of bias** — Tools used (Cochrane RoB-2, ROBINS-I, CASP, JBI), independent review process
- **Data synthesis** — Meta-analysis models (fixed/random effects), heterogeneity assessment (I²), subgroup analyses
- **Timeline & status** — Completed vs ongoing, publication intentions
- **Team & affiliations** — Contact, funding, COI declarations

## How to Use

1. **Browse** the topic closest to your own SR/MA question
2. **Read** the full PROSPERO record in each folder
3. **Model** your own protocol on the PICO structure, search strategy, and synthesis plan
4. **Adapt** the outcome measures, databases, and risk-of-bias tools to your specific topic
