## Key Takeaways

The guide covers 11 protocol sections with aesthetic dermatology-specific examples:
1. **Title** — Three viable formats, 250-char limit, the meta-analysis over-promise trap
2. **Rationale** — Three-paragraph structure, the "gap trap" (weak vs strong justifications when 2 SRs exist)
3. **Objectives** — SMART objectives, primary vs secondary sentence starters
4. **Eligibility Criteria** — Full inclusion/exclusion table, split-face definition, three-tier formulation classification
5. **Search Strategy** — Database tier justification, MeSH verification, search filters, language honesty
6. **Outcomes** — Primary/secondary hierarchy, instrument hierarchy, outcome-time matrix
7. **Data Extraction** — Essential fields, paired data handling, SMD from paired designs formulas
8. **Risk of Bias** — Tool selection rationale table, RoB 2 adaptations for within-patient designs
9. **Data Synthesis** — Meta-analysis decision tree, I² interpretation with prediction intervals, SWiM operationalisation
10. **Timeline** — Realistic durations per stage, "12 months from registration" safe estimate
11. **Team / COI** — Guarantor role, contributions table, COI phrasing
