A comprehensive reference document compiling methodological guidance for writing realistic, defensible, and publication-ready PROSPERO systematic review protocols in aesthetic dermatology.

## Contents

1. **PRISMA-P 2015 Checklist** — Full 17-item checklist (27 sub-items) with per-item critical notes. Sources: Shamseer et al. BMJ 2015 (doi:10.1136/bmj.g7647), Moher et al. Syst Rev 2015 (doi:10.1186/2046-4053-4-1)

2. **Cochrane Handbook** — Synthesis of Chapter II (Planning), Chapter 1 (Starting), Chapter 2 (Scope), Chapter 23 (Variants on RCTs — crossover/split-face). Version 6.5 (2024), with exact chapter URLs.

3. **MECIR Standards** — Mandatory (M) and Highly Desirable (C) standards mapped to protocol items. MECIR M1-M8 and C2-C38.

4. **PROSPERO Requirements vs Optional** — Complete 22-field mapping to PRISMA-P items. What's mandatory vs optional.

5. **Common PROSPERO Submission Errors** — 20 documented errors in 3 severity tiers (Critical/Moderate/Low).

6. **Aesthetic Dermatology SR Best Practices** — Database tiering, outcome measures (GAIS, FACE-Q, Cutometer, MASI), key publication venues, methodological considerations.

7. **Split-Face Trials Methodology** — Eligibility, data extraction (within-patient correlation, paired statistics), analysis (generic inverse-variance from paired differences vs. imputation via correlation coefficient), RoB adaptation.

8. **Risk of Bias Tools by Study Design** — RoB 2 (Sterne 2019), ROBINS-I (Sterne 2016), RoB 2 crossover adaptation (Handbook Ch.23 Table 23.2.a), NIH/NHLBI/JBI for case series. Tiered approach.

9. **GRADE for Aesthetic Outcomes** — Standard 5-domain framework + 4 specific challenges: patient-reported vs clinician-reported outcomes, split-face correlation inflating precision, surrogate vs patient-important outcomes, small sample sizes.

10. **Lampridou 2025 PN Review Structure Analysis** — Full analysis of the existing PN SR (PMID: 39645667, DOI: 10.1111/jocd.16721). Strengths and 7 identified improvement areas for future protocols.

11. **Key Citations** — 15 core references with DOIs.

12. **URL Index** — 28 verified sources (20 methodology + 8 exemplar papers).
